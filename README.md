# MiniMusix

### A music companion for macOS that keeps your music, lyrics, and controls right where you need them.

MiniMusix is designed for people who want quick access to their music without opening a full music app every time they want to check a song, view lyrics, switch devices, or manage playback.

Built around a compact floating player, MiniMusix focuses on making music feel more connected to your desktop while staying out of the way when you don't need it.

---

# Features

## 🎵 Music at a Glance

See what's playing, control playback, and keep track of your music without interrupting what you're doing.

## 🎨 Artwork That Shapes the Experience

MiniMusix adapts to the artwork of the song you're listening to, creating a unique look and feel for every track.

## 📝 Lyrics

Follow along with your favorite songs through an integrated lyrics experience designed to stay within easy reach.

## 🌌 Ambient Mode

Turn your current song into an immersive visual experience inspired by its artwork and colors.

## 🔊 Audio Device Controls

Quickly switch between speakers, headphones, Bluetooth devices, and other available listening options.

## 📍 Menu Bar Access

Access MiniMusix directly from your menu bar whenever you need it.

## ⚙️ Personalization

Adjust the appearance and behavior of MiniMusix to match your preferences.

---

# Why MiniMusix?

Many music players are built around large windows and crowded interfaces.

MiniMusix takes a different approach.

The goal is to provide the information and controls you use most often while keeping your desktop clean and focused. Whether you're studying, working, gaming, or simply listening to music, MiniMusix is designed to stay close by without demanding attention.
